1. Your status tracker

Sign in to your Application Status Tracker account

This tracker lets you check the status of certain immigration and citizenship applications. Before using it, make sure it supports your application.

Session timeout: After 60 minutes of inactivity, your session will expire and you will be automatically signed out.

Check the status of your application

  • If you're a sponsor or principal applicant, enter your unique client identifier (UCI) or client ID and password.
  • If you're a representative or designated individual, enter your party ID and password.
